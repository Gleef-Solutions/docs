<Tip>You can now create a demo account and try Gleef for free. [Try it now](http://get.gleef.eu)</Tip>

## Sign Up
Follow these steps to quickly set up a demo account if your company hasn't registered yet.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the Chrome Extension">
    Start by [downloading our Chrome extension](http://get.gleef.eu).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up using either your email or Google account to register in just a few seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Your Email">
    After registration, you'll need to confirm your email to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register Your Company">
    Once logged into the extension, you'll be prompted to define your company and its domain. Ensure that you use the domain you'd like to experiment with.

    <Warning>If the domain is already registered by another user, you will not be able to create an account. Please [contact us](mailto:support@gleef.eu) if you have any questions.</Warning>

    If you have any questions regarding your company's domain usage and how we manage multiple users, please refer to our [Q&A](TK).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Using Gleef">
    You're all set to start using Gleef as a demo user. You can create experiments, but they will remain drafts and won't affect your website until you upgrade your account.

    <Info>Want your experiments to go live? You can upgrade your account at any time by [contacting us](mailto:support@gleef.eu).</Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>
